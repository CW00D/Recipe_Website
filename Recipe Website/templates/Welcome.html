What do we do?

Lee's Recipe Book is a platform that we've created to promote the sharing of great recipes and to help people find a new hobby they never though they could have. Promoting local chefs and helping others find their flair. Help us by becoming a member if you arent already and find many great recipes or sign up as a chef and share your expertise.
